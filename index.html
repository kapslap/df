What is Effective Choice?

Effective Choice (AEC) is a practical ethics for clear, life-affirming decisions. It clarifies how desire, want, and need relate, maps basal motivations, and distinguishes choices that benefit self and world without collapsing into moralism. It’s metaphysics translated into everyday discernment—communication, action, responsibility, justice, and maturity.

Season 1 of the podcast unfolds the AEC aphorisms conversationally: Forrest teaches; Jared questions and reflects; understanding gets sculpted in real time.

FAQ

When does the first cohort start?

As soon as the waitlist reaches 10–20 learners. We’ll email everyone on the list with dates and details.

What’s the commitment?

Weekly sessions for the duration of the cohort, plus optional practice labs and a modest amount of exercises between sessions.

Do I need prior background in philosophy?

No. Curiosity and a willingness to practice are sufficient.

How is the course different from the podcast?

The podcast introduces and explores AEC conversationally. The course is a guided, small-group practice environment with live feedback and applied exercises.

Where do the materials live?

Course materials are shared with participants; the AEC canon remains Forrest’s IP. Content created together (podcast and course materials) is jointly held by Delicate Fire LLC.
